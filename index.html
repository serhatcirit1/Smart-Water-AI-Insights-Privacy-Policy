Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how your information is collected when you use Smart Water: AI Insights ("the App").

1. Information Collection and Use

Most of your data is stored locally on your device only.

1.1. Non-Personal Data

  • Daily water intake, goals, and reminder preferences.
  • User profile (Weight, activity - used for local calculations only).

1.2. Health Data (Apple Health)

  • Data Writing: Data is written to Apple Health only with your explicit permission.
  • Privacy: Health data is never used for advertising purposes.

3. Third-Party Services (RevenueCat)

Purchases are managed via RevenueCat. Your credit card information is not stored by us.
