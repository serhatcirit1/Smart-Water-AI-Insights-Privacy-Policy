Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how your information is collected, used, and shared when you use Smart Water: AI Insights ("the App"). Your privacy is important to us.

1. Information Collection and Use

Our app collects minimal data, and most of your data is stored locally on your device only.

1.1. Non-Personal Data

  • Daily water intake and goals
  • Reminder preferences and app settings
  • User profile (Weight, activity level - used for local calculations only)

1.2. Health Data (Apple Health / HealthKit)

  • Data Writing: Data is written to Apple Health only if you grant explicit permission.
  • Privacy: Health data is never used for advertising or shared with third parties.

2. Third-Party Services (RevenueCat)

Premium features and subscriptions are managed via RevenueCat. Your payment details are processed by Apple/Google and are not visible to us.
